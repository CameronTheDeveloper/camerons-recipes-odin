Potato Salad recipe

Details

Looking for a healthy and convenient recipe that also tastes good? Look no further than our homemade Potato Salad recipe. Our potato salad brings back the traditional ingredients this delicacy was made with such as mixing Eggs, Celery, Onion, and Mayonnaise. Enjoy this meal for breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (4)
  • Mayonnaise (3/8 cup)
  • Eggs (3)
  • Onion (1/3 cup)
  • Relish (1/3 cup)
  • Mustard (3/4 tablespoon)
  • Seasoning of your choice (recommended)

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes for 12-15 minutes.
  2. Boil eggs for 8-10 minutes.
  3. Mix potatoes, eggs, onion, mayonnaise, mustard, relish, and celery.
  4. Chill salad in the refrigerator to serve cold.
