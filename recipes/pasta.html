Browned Butter Pasta recipe

Details

Our favorite buttery delicacy is quick and easy to make in your kitchen with just a handful of ingredients. This mouthwatering dish is created by adding melted butter onto cooked pasta of your choice. Mix the butter and pasta with our recommendation of parmesan or your choice of cheese. You may also add to the explosion of flavor with gourmet pasta toppings and/or seasoning of your choice.

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • Pasta (of any style)
  • Butter (12-16 tablespoons)
  • Parmesan cheese (recommended)
  • Your favorite toppings/seasoning (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil 4 quarts of water.
  2. Add a pinch of salt to water.
  3. Add Pasta and boil for 11-12 minutes.
  4. Melt butter on a skillet with medium heat.
  5. Once butter is melted to golden brown texture, mix with cooked pasta.
  6. Add cheese and other toppings.
